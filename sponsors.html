Sponsorships

For over five decades, CLCF Baseball has offered fun and competitive baseball programs to players ranging from ages 4 – 12 throughout the City of Cranston. We have over 400 players participate annually at our five fields across Cranston.

We strive for player development in a healthy, family-oriented program. Our success on the field has hed to numerous state championships in Cal Ripken Baseball.

We depend greatly on your assistance as our sponsors to serve our City, families, and players. Your sponsorship will enhance our programs to your youth, as well as offer your business exposure throughout our league. Below you will find the current sponsor options as well as the pdf packet to apply!

TEAM SPONSORS: $500
If you choose to sponsor a team, you get your business name on Hats and Jerseys that team’s players will wear throughout the season. Your AD will be posted on the CLCF WEBSITE and you will also receive a Team photo and plaque.
FIELD SIGN: $250
You will receive a 4’ by 8’ vinyl sign that will be displayed on your field of choice. Additional signs may be purchased at $200 per sign or you can purchase for all 4 CLCF Fields for $750.

MULTI-TEAM, EMERALD, AND GOLD SPONSORSHIP *NEW*
FOUR-TEAM SPONSOR: $2000 This option receives a FIELD SIGN at all 4 CLCF Fields, TEAM SPONSORS (1 team in each division) with their choice of team color, an AD on the CLCF WEBSITE and all CLCF SOCIAL MEDIA PAGES and a TEAM PHOTO with PLAQUES
EMERALD SPONSOR: $800
This option receives a FIELD SIGN at all 4 CLCF Fields, a TEAM SPONSOR with their choice of team color, an AD on the CLCF WEBSITE and all CLCF SOCIAL MEDIA PAGES and a TEAM PHOTO with PLAQUE
GOLD SPONSOR: $700
This option receives a FIELD SIGN at your CLCF Field or choice, a TEAM SPONSOR with their choice of team color, an AD on the CLCF WEBSITE and all CLCF SOCIAL MEDIA PAGES and a TEAM PHOTO with PLAQUE

Interested in sponsoring? Email us at baseball@clcfsports.org, or click here to download a sponsorship packet.

Donate

Thank you for considering a donation to enhance the CLCF baseball experience. Your generosity directly contributes to the improvement of our league, ensuring a vibrant and enjoyable environment for players, families, and spectators alike. Donations are crucial in providing exciting jersey and hat options for teams, making necessary improvements to our fields, including re-sodding and new windscreens due to weather deterioration. Additionally, your support helps enhance player experience with improved dugouts, offering a safe and organized space for equipment and cheering on teammates. Upgrades to bleacher and concession equipment benefit families and spectators attending our games.

Interested in Donating? Email us at baseball@clcfsports.org, or click here to download a sponsorship packet.
