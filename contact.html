Email Info

All email addresses that are indicated on a player's registration form will be entered in our email database, and will be signed up to receive league-wide email blasts. You can unsubscribe from this list by clicking "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any mass email. If you are not receiving emails from CLCF Baseball, but would like to, please email us at contact@clcfbaseball.com with your name and child's playing division, and we will add your email to the appropriate list.

Social Media Info

In the event that we need to distribute important information (such as games being cancelled due to weather) we will post updates on (1) clcfbaseball.com (2) Facebook and (3) Twitter. Social media is the best way to receive real-time updates from CLCF Baseball, so please be sure that you follow us.
