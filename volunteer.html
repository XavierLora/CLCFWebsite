Volunteering

CLCF Baseball is run by hard-working volunteers who are dedicated to supporting youth baseball in the City of Cranston.

No person may volunteer in any capacity in CLCF Baseball without a background check. Background checks must be run through CLCF Baseball (background checks run through a job, child's school, etc. are not acceptable.)

If you are interested in volunteering with CLCF Baseball, please click here to download a Background Check Disclaimer Form*. Please mail the completed form, a copy of a photo ID (with date of birth), and a $5.00 processing fee to: CLCF Baseball, 970 Pontiac Ave. Cranston 02920.

*This form is required for any person who wishes to volunteer in any capacity with CLCF Baseball. Please fill out this form up to the "Applicant Signature." The form must be notarized (you may submit the form without notary authorization, and it will be notarized by a league official.)
