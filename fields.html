Our Fields

.

CLCF Baseball runs practices and games at a variety of fields throughout the City of Cranston. Please click on the name of a field for more information about the field, as well as directions to the field. Please note that per city ordinance, smoking is not allowed at any field.

Hope Field

460 Hope Road Cranston, Rhode Island 02921

In the John Chafee Athletic Complex, Cranston's first 50'70 baseball field, renovated in 2012, supports both 50'70 and traditional 40'60 games, with a batting cage for players and seating/restrooms for spectators.

Ricci "Bain" Field

20 Duckworth Street Cranston, Rhode Island 02920

CLCF Baseball's central field, near Hugh B. Bain Middle School, hosts tournaments, including the 2013 Cal Ripken Regional. It offers player bullpens, a concession stand, seating, and restrooms for spectators.

Garden City Field

34 Magazine Street Cranston, Rhode Island, 02920

Located behind Garden City Elementary School, this field has a batting cage and bullpen for players, as well as seating for spectators. Garden City Field was renovated in December of 2015, and a new grass infield was installed.

CLCF Building Field

970 Pontiac Avenue Cranston, Rhode Island, 02920

Located behind the CLCF Building, this field is home to our younger divisions. Offers a baseball field, and open land for practices, as well as seating for spectators.
